Frequently Asked Questions

Is the advance amount refundable?
No, the advance amount is not refundable as it secures your booking date and time slot.
When should I book my mehendi appointment?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during wedding seasons. For popular dates, booking 1-2 months ahead is advisable.
Do you provide mehndi services outside Lucknow?
Yes, we provide mehndi services outside Lucknow with additional travel charges depending on the location and accommodation requirements.
Are the mehendi colours natural and safe?
Absolutely! We use only natural, high-quality mehendi that is safe for all skin types. Our colors are free from harmful chemicals and provide a rich, dark stain.
How long does the mehendi application take?
Bridal mehendi typically takes 3-5 hours depending on the complexity of design. Guest mehendi takes about 30-60 minutes per person.
